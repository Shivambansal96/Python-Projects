Grocery Shop Billing System

Manage inventory, generate bills, apply discounts, and store purchase history

Overview

This system helps grocery shops manage their inventory, create bills for customers, apply discounts, and maintain sales records.

  1. Add or update products in inventory.
  2. Generate bills for customer purchases.
  3. Apply discounts on eligible products.
  4. View daily sales summaries.

Data is stored in inventory.csv for products and transactions.json for sales.

Detailed Specification

Objective

Manage shop inventory and billing.

Core Classes

  • Product – name, price, stock.
  • Bill – purchased products, total.
  • Inventory – manages product stock.

Core Features

  • Add/update products.
  • Add items to cart and calculate total.
  • Apply discounts.

Example Flow

  1. Add “Rice” – ₹50/kg, stock 20kg.
  2. Customer buys 2kg → ₹100.
  3. Stock reduces to 18kg.

1. Inventory Management

Manage products with details such as:

  • Product ID – Unique identifier (e.g., PR001).
  • Name – Product name.
  • Price – Price per unit.
  • Stock – Quantity available.

Stored in inventory.csv.

2. Billing

Billing process:

  1. Add items to cart.
  2. Calculate total cost.
  3. Generate final bill with breakdown.

Bill details are recorded in transactions.json.

3. Discount Module

Discount features include:

  • Apply product-specific offers.
  • Percentage-based or flat-rate discounts.
  • Automatic discount calculation in billing.

4. Reports

Generate reports for:

  • Total sales for the day.
  • Top-selling products.
  • Total revenue generated.

File Structures

inventory.csv

product_id,name,price,stock PR001,Rice,50,100 PR002,Milk,25,50

transactions.json

[ { "transaction_id": "T001", "items": [ {"product": "Rice", "quantity": 2, "price": 100}, {"product": "Milk", "quantity": 1, "price": 25} ], "total": 125, "date": "2025-08-13" } ]
